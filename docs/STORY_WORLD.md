# Story & World Document

## Core Concept
Foundation Academy is an abandoned academy-city where mathematical rules control the world. The world is built of blocky geometry. The "Fraction Reservoir" district stores energy in divided reservoirs. The exit gate is locked because reservoir energy ratios are inconsistent.

## Characters
- **Player**: Unnamed explorer.
- **Lumi**: A small system voice that provides short text hints. Lumi does not solve the puzzle for the player.

## Opening Message
“Foundation Academy detected unstable reservoir ratios. Restore the correct valves to unlock the exit.”

## Implementation Rules
- Minimal environmental storytelling.
- No long cutscenes.
- Short text hints only.
