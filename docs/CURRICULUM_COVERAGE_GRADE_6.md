# Curriculum Coverage - Grade 6

## Covered Nodes
For the MVP and Loop 2, the following Grade 6 nodes are covered:
1. `g6_fraction_recognize`: Recognize fractions from visual representations (Status: appliedInMap).
2. `g6_fraction_equivalent`: Identify equivalent fractions (Status: appliedInMap).
3. `g6_fraction_compare`: Compare fractions to determine the largest/smallest (Status: appliedInMap).
4. `g6_number_natural_numbers`: Understand natural and whole numbers (Status: introduced).
5. `g6_number_multiples`: Identify and generate multiples of a number (Status: appliedInMap).
6. `g6_number_factors`: Identify factors of a number (Status: appliedInMap).
7. `g6_number_common_factors`: Find common factors and greatest common factor (Status: introduced).
8. `g6_number_divisibility_logic`: Apply divisibility rules (Status: introduced).
9. `g6_number_prime_composite`: Classify numbers as prime or composite (Status: appliedInMap).

## Implementation Rules
- Do not claim full Grade 6 coverage. Nodes must state their actual coverageStatus (introduced, practiced, appliedInMap, assessed, challengeAvailable).
- Each appliedInMap node maps to specific puzzles or tiers (e.g. `g6_fraction_reservoir_01`, and the new Primes Plaza area).
